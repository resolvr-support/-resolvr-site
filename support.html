Frequently Asked Questions

How do I cancel my subscription?

Open the iPhone Settings app → tap your name → Subscriptions → select Resolvr → Cancel Subscription.

How do I restore a previous purchase?

Open Resolvr → Profile tab → tap Restore Purchases at the top of the paywall.

My AI insight isn't loading.

Check your internet connection. If it persists, force-quit and reopen the app, or email us with the decision you were trying to make.

Is my data private?

Yes. Your decisions are stored locally on your device. See our Privacy Policy for details.

How do I delete my data?

All data is stored locally. Delete the Resolvr app to remove everything.
