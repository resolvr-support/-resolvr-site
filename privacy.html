Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 16, 2026

Resolvr ("we", "our", "the app") is designed with privacy in mind. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app handles and how.

Information We Collect

Resolvr does not require you to create an account. Your decision history, preferences, and settings are stored locally on your device.

  • Decision Inputs: The questions and options you enter are processed to generate guidance. When you use AI-powered features (Resolvr Insight, Refine), the text of your decision is sent securely to our AI processing provider to generate a response, and is not stored on our servers beyond the request.
  • Subscription Data: If you purchase Resolvr Pro, subscription status is managed by Apple and RevenueCat. We do not see your payment information.
  • Diagnostics: Apple may share anonymous crash diagnostics with us if you opt in via iOS Settings. This contains no personal data.

What We Don't Do

  • We do not sell your data.
  • We do not use third-party advertising trackers.
  • We do not build a profile of you.
  • We do not require sign-up or email.

Third-Party Services

  • RevenueCat: Handles subscription receipts. See RevenueCat Privacy.
  • AI Provider: AI requests are routed through our secure proxy to generate guidance. Inputs are not retained for training.
  • Apple: Subscriptions and IAP are processed by Apple.

Your Choices

You can delete your local decision history at any time from within the app. Deleting the app removes all local data.

Children

Resolvr is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes

If we update this policy, we'll update the "Last updated" date above.
